Live Honey Bee Removal and Relocation

Safe and Professional Live Honey Bee Removal and Relocation

We specialize in the safe and professional removal and relocation of live Honey bees from a variety of locations, including roofs, walls, trees, water meter boxes, and structures made of wood, concrete, or steel.

During the removal process, we carefully extract the bees along with their vital resources such as wax comb, stored pollen, honey, and developing brood (baby bees). Once removed, we relocate the colony to a local apiary where we ensure their survival and provide them with a new home, allowing them to continue their essential role in pollination.

Whether dealing with a temporary swarm of bees or an established colony, we are fully equipped to handle both situations with the utmost care and professionalism. Swarms, which are temporary gatherings of bees in search of a new home, and established colonies, with their permanent nests, both require specialized techniques for safe removal and relocation.

As a fully registered and insured service, we are committed to providing the highest level of professionalism and safety. We use essential tools and techniques designed for safe and effective bee removal, ensuring minimal disruption to your property while protecting the bees from harm. Our priority is to safeguard your safety and the preservation of these vital pollinators, offering a humane solution for bee removal.
